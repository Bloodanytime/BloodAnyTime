Blood Center is public donation center with blood donation members in the changing health care system. Founded in 1978, Blood Center is one of the nations oldest and largest nonprofit transfusion medicine organizations. We provide a blood and volunteer services across the US. With our national footprint, deep community roots and specialized services, we are the thread that connects people and resources together to fuel progress in transfusion medicine.

  • Inspiring people to give blood
  • Specialist blood donors and clinical supervision.
  • Producing a safe and ready blood supply
  • Increasing communication with our members.
  • High quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment.
  • Offering specialized patient services and pharmaceuticals
